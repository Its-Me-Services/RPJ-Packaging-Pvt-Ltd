About Us

We have over 30 years of experience

Who we are

We are an ISO 9001 : 2015 certified company, founded in 1989 and is currently under the directorship of R.P. Jhunjhunwala and Deepak Jhunjhunwala, who are working around the clock to keep the company up to date with the market and client requirements.

RPJ is one of the industry leaders and provides aluminium foil products to clients after rigorous research to provide the best quality products with cutting edge technology. We cater to various clients throughout India, along with Bangladesh and Nepal.

What we do

We cater to various industries which include pharmaceuticals, food packaging, cosmetics and industrial goods. Our products are as per the legal guidelines of the business operations of the client.

We use advanced technology in our production processes while meeting the safety compliance of the government. RPJ adheres to the environmental guidelines as well in our manufacturing plants to ensure social safety.

How we do it

With our experience over the span of more than thirty years in the industry, our team works tirelessly to innovate and push the limits of the organisation and helping us acquire a global status. We encourage individuality to enhance employee motivation and garner better employee commitment which help us move forward in a mutually benefitting environment.

We are a closely knit manufacturing unit working together to supply the clients with superior quality products maintaining the quality standards of the company.

Our products for your business growth

We provide wide range of products such as printed aluminum blister foils, HSL lidding foils, pouches, induction sealing wads for industries like pharmaceuticals, food & beverages and others. We also deliver customized products as per client specific demands and requirements.

We provide customer care services as well to ensure complete client satisfaction and encourage valuable feedback which is useful for our growth and client relationship. RPJ remains committed to its vision of providing the best quality at the right price at right time.

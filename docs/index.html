Get to know about RPJ

RPJ is one of the leading premium aluminium foil manufacturers in India. Based in Tangra, Kolkata, it has been founded by Raghunad Prasad Jhunjhunwala in 1989.

Our company offers a wide variety of aluminium products to serve to the changing demands of the clients over course of time and have successfully overcome industry challenges for more than three decades.
